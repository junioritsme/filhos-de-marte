The Book

About the Story

Centuries after humanity's first steps on Mars, the red planet holds secrets that predate our arrival. Beneath the rust-colored dunes and ancient craters lie relics of a truth so profound, it threatens to reshape everything we believe about our origins.

When archaeologist Dr. Elena Vasquez discovers an impossible artifact buried deep within Valles Marineris, she sets in motion a chain of events that will pit science against faith, empires against outcasts, and the children of Earth against the children of Mars.

"We came to Mars seeking a new beginning. Instead, we found an ending that had been waiting for us for millennia."

— From the Archives of the First Expedition

The Writer

About the Author

A lifelong student of the cosmos and the human condition, I have spent years contemplating our place among the stars. "Filhos de Marte" is the culmination of these meditations—a story that asks the questions I have always been afraid to answer.

This is not a story of heroes and villains, but of seekers and the truths they find. I invite you to join them on their journey across the red sands of Mars, into the depths of mysteries older than humanity itself.

— The Author
